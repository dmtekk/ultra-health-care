Our Services

We offer a wide range of medical services, all bulk billed with a valid Medicare card, ensuring that your health comes first with no out-of-pocket expenses. Our clinic is open six days a week by appointment, so you can receive the care you need when you need it most.

General Practitioners

Our GPs are committed to providing high quality affordable care for all our patients. Your long-term wellbeing is our primary goal.

Skin Cancer Tests

Skin cancer checks are completed by Dr Siapantas. They are non invasive and are the best way to ensure early detection of skin cancer. The sooner a skin cancer is identified and treated, the better the prognosis. These can be booked online or by calling the clinic. We offer bulk billing for simple mole checks as well as full body screening. We recommend that you get a full body check once every 12 months

Pathology

We have Dorevitch Pathology located onsite. All pathology requires a request form from a GP. No appointment is required to see the pathology nurse. Patients are seen in the order of arrival.

Immunisations

Immunisations are a simple, safe and effective way to protect children from harmful diseases. We provide bulk billed paediatric and childhood immunisations.

Womens Health

Our doctors have a wealth of experience in dealing with women’s health issues including family planning and contraception, cervical screening (Pap tests), gynaecological concerns, mental health, preventative health and chronic disease management.

Audiology

Free hearing tests are available with our audiologist Steven Kargas. You’ll be set up in a quiet screening room for your hearing test. You'll be played a series of sounds through a pair of headphones at four different frequencies. You simply have to press a button or raise your hand each time you hear a sound. Hearing tests are BULK BILLED and can be done from ages 8 months.
