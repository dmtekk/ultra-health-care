About us

Ultra Health Care

Ultra Health Care is your one-stop medical centre. Located opposite the Southland Village Cinemas, our GPs have been providing quality health care for over 30 years. We are an appointment-based clinic open six days a week.Medical appointments with a GP are bulk billed provided that you hold a valid Medicare card which means there is no out-of-pocket cost. Pathology and hearing tests are available on site and are also bulk billed. Our other specialists include a dietician and a psychologist.

Our Qualified Team is Always Ready to Help You!

Our growing medical and allied health team aims to provide the highest level of health care and service to our patients.
