General Practitioners

Our GPs are committed to providing high quality affordable care for all our patients. Your long-term wellbeing is our primary goal.

Skin Cancer Tests

Skin cancer checks are completed by Dr Siapantas. They are non invasive and are the best way to ensure early detection of skin cancer. The sooner a skin cancer is identified and treated, the better the prognosis. These can be booked online or by calling the clinic. We offer bulk billing for simple mole checks as well as full body screening. We recommend that you get a full body check once every 12 months

Pathology

We have Dorevitch Pathology located onsite. All pathology requires a request form from a GP. No appointment is required to see the pathology nurse. Patients are seen in the order of arrival.
